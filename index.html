About Me

Hello! my name is Karl Ross Gironda. I am a 34 year old coding student at Eleven Fifty Academy in Fishers, Indiana. Currently I am working on my very first version of my portfolio for future employers to look at my accomplishments and consider me to be a future candidate. I will be including my projects for the first 3 weeks of our course showcasing my skills. It has been a challenge to be submersed in a bootcamp style of learning but I enjoy not wasting time so it works out really well for me. My goals are to act like a sponge and take on all the information I can, all while practicing to better myself so I can prepare for my future as a Web Developer. I have been working in the monotony of sales for the past 10 years and im ready for the personal and professional growth that this type of work can fulfull for me. I was born in New Orleans, Louisiana; home to a very family oreinted and neighborly way of life. Growing up in the south has that benefit of making its habitants very empathetic and non-chalant individuals with a flare for being individualistic.
New Orleans is very rich with culture and history, which is engraved in my spirit as well as my personal style. Classic nostalgia blended with the vibrance of color and personality is very much a design culture I live and design by. Design and creating run in my family which is what draws me to digital architecture/design. My Aunt Kellie is a artist and costume designer for anything from Mardi Gras costumes to murals and my Father worked in innovations at Luxxotica for 30 years.
Currently I live in downtown Indianapolis, Indiana with my husband Kyle and our two yorkies Remy(7) and Kane(5). Some of our hobbies include traveling, dining out, driving our little red corvette and football. Oddly enough there is a funny connection between where I come from and where I live now, Drew Brees the quarterback for the New Orleans Saints went to Purdue a prominent engineering college in Lafayette, Indiana. I would consider myself a foodie at heart and love to try any new restaurant that opens up! I love food so much that I decided to do my first API project as a recipe search. I hope you enjoy my portfolio adn learning a little bit about myself. If you have any questions or feedback I would greatly appreciated it, as this portfolio will constantly be changing and advancing as my skills do.
